	CHARACTERISTICS
Intellect - show how clever character is. Small affect on prices.
Agility - show how agile character is. Small affect on chance to damage enemy, big affect on chance to avoid enemy attack, stamina bonus.
Strength && Martial arts - show how strong character is and how is it good at killing anyone. Big affect on chance to damage enemy, small affect on chance to avoid enemy attack, HP bonus.
Charizma - show how good character is in talking to other creatures. Medium affect on prices.
Luck - show how lucky character is. Affects on chance of positive result of event and random positive event chance.
