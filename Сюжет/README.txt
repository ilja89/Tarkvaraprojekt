Green arrows are for choise\event what can be performed once, it shouldn't be an option in second case.
Bright blue arrows are for choises\events what must be performed, if there are no more other choises, usually mustn't be accessible.
Purple are lines with chance of happening, what calculates first and independently on other lines. If this line didn't happen, calculates chance for other lines, as if there are no this purple line.
Other colors are just to make diagram more understandable.
Lines what are not marked with chance of happening, are choises.


Characteristic symbols:
I - Intellect
A - Agility
S - Strength && Martial arts
C - Charizma
L - Luck
